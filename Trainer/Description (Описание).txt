For english:
This trainer will help you have fun in the game or help you complete tasks in your career.
The hotkey corresponds to the effect.

F1 - Unlimited NOS.
F2 - Unlimited time dilation.
F3 - Ghost mode.
F4 - Remove rivals in the race.
F5 - Removes the passage of races.
F6 - Removes the passage of bets.
F7 - Removes the passage of bounty.
F8 - No blow engine.
F9 - No arrest.
F10 - Invulnerable to thorns.
F11 - Getting away from the cops.
F12 - Autopilot (Drag race).
Alt+F10 - Do not wait for the start countdown.
Alt+F11 - Increase the timer (for time racing).
Alt+F12 - Autopilot.
V - Super speed.

Для русских:
Данный трейнер поможет весело провести время в игре или поможет пройти задания в карьере.
Горячая клавиша соответствует эффекту.
